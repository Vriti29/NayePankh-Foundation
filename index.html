Who We Are

A movement built by students, for communities.

NayePankh — meaning "new wings" — empowers young people to become changemakers. We connect students and professionals with meaningful internships and volunteering, while running grassroots drives that uplift underserved communities across India.

  • ✓ Government registered, 80G & 12A certified
  • ✓ Industry-aligned internships with certificates
  • ✓ Real community impact at scale
  • ✓ 100% transparency on every contribution

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for an internship?+

Simply fill out the application form in the "Get Involved" section above, choose your preferred track, and our team will reach out with the next steps.

Is a certificate provided?+

Yes. Every intern who successfully completes their program receives a recognised completion certificate from NayePankh Foundation.

Can beginners apply?+

Absolutely. Our programs are designed to support all skill levels with mentorship and structured guidance, so beginners are welcome.

Are the internships remote?+

Most internships are fully remote, so you can contribute from anywhere in India. Select volunteer drives are conducted on-ground.
